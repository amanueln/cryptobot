UNDER THE HOOD

Where the data comes from, where it lands

Three live feeds from Coinbase power the whole thing. Every decision is written back to disk for later analysis — that's how we know if a trade was a real signal or just noise.
Hourly candles
REST poll at :00
→ momentum score → gate log → regime_snapshots + candles
WS ticker
per-trade feed
→ peak / trough → trail stop → momentum_trades (MAE/MFE)
WS L2 book
50 levels, 5s snap
→ top-5 bids → wall detector → wall-aware trail → wall_decisions + l2_snapshots
WS matches
every fill on watched pairs
→ flow pressure → ws_matches (for post-trade analysis — was the wall real flow or spoofing?)
A FULL TRADE, MINUTE BY MINUTE

One day. One trade. Start to finish.

This is what the last Friday's SOL trade actually looked like — synthesized from the real logs.
10:00:00 — HOURLY CLOSE
Scanner wakes up
New hourly candle closes. Engine recomputes momentum for all 10 pairs. Candidates ranked by acceleration. SOL-USD leads at +34%.
10:00:02 — GATES
Quality + structure filters
6 of 10 coins fail on accel. 2 more fail structure (AVAX at ATH, DOGE too old). SOL and LINK survive. Reasons logged to _gate_log.
10:00:03 — REGIME
BTC meta-check
BTC at $68,420. Regime MA at $67,015. Above by 2.1%, well past the 0.5% hysteresis band. Regime = bullish → proceed. Snapshot saved to regime_snapshots.
10:00:04 — ENTRY
Market buy SOL-USD @ $142.00
$3,000 allocation → 21.13 SOL. Fee: $9. Entry row written to momentum_trades. WS subscribes to SOL ticker + L2. Book-writer thread starts pushing live state at 1 Hz. Peak = trough = $142.00.
10:40 — LOCK-IN #1
+6% hit → hard floor
Price at $150.52. Progressive lock activates. Floor raised to entry × 1.012 = $143.70. Exit now guaranteed green, even if it reverses.
11:10 — LOCK-IN #2 + WALL
+8% fires, L2 wall spotted
Price at $153.36. Floor jumps to $147.70 (+4%). Meanwhile L2 shows an $18k bid wall at $150 — size is 6× our position, aged 12s. Wall qualifies → stop snaps up to $149.85. wall_decisions row written.
11:30 — PEAK
High-water at $156.30
MFE (max favorable excursion): +3.03%. Stored on the trade row. Price starts to fade.
12:30 — EXIT
Price drops through wall-aware stop
Price prints $149.80, one tick below the stop. Market sell. 21.13 SOL → $3,165. Net P&L after both-side fees: +$147 (+4.9%). Exit reason: wall_aware_stop. Full snapshot (peak, trough, ticks_since_peak, MAE/MFE) finalized.
12:30–13:00 — COOLDOWN
30-minute timeout
Engine refuses new entries for 30m to avoid whipsaw. Hero bar shows cash only. Status banner displays "Cooldown · 28m left".
13:00 — LOOP
Next hourly close
Scanner wakes up again. Back to step 1. Over a week, this loop runs ~168 times — of those, maybe 3–6 trigger actual entries.
